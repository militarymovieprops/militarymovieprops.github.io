About Us

Your Vision, Our Vehicles.

We are a family-owned business located in Georgia that specializes in providing various types of military vehicles, including current issue Humvees, as well as miscellaneous military set props, JDM right-hand drive vehicles, police SWAT vehicles, and more to the movie industry. We offer custom builds, fabrication, and paint services on-site to meet your specific needs.

As a Georgia-born resident, we take great pride in our work and vehicles. Our small team of dedicated professionals, including myself and my two sons, are committed to ensuring that every vehicle is meticulously maintained and ready for the show.

If you don't see what you need in our inventory, we are always willing to try and locate it for you. Our goal is to provide exceptional service and quality vehicles that will help bring your creative vision to life on the big screen. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help with your military vehicle and set prop needs.
